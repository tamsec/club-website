Our Mission

WiCyS is a community of engagement, encouragement and support for women in cybersecurity. Women in CyberSecurity (WiCyS) is the premier organization with a national reach dedicated to bringing together women in cybersecurity from academia, research and industry to share knowledge, experience, networking and mentoring.
