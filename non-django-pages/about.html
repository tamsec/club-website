About Us

Texas A&M Cybersecurity Club is a student-led and student-run organization under the support of Texas A&M Cybersecurity Center. Our goal is to develop and encourage cybersecurity skills and awareness to students willing to learn. No experience is necessary so come join our meetings and events to learn, network, compete, and have fun. Meetings are free and open to all Texas A&M students.
