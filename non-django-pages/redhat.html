What Is Red Hat Academy?

The Red Hat Academy is an academic training program developed by Red Hat to bridge the gap between industry and education by partnering with educational institutions. At Texas A&M, the Cybersecurity Center has partnered with Red Hat to help students acquire enterprise-ready skills and provide learning material that may not be regularly taught in an academic curriculum. The Red Hat Academy curriculum focuses on enterprise Linux and open source technologies.

Why Join?

The program focuses on teaching relevant and in-demand skills to further your career and job prospects. With more than 90% of the global fortune 500 companies using red hat products and solutions, this program can differential yourself from other students by learning material that may not have been traditionally taught through academia. We plan to provide valuable experience through hands-on labs that will help you towards earning a certification and building more confidence in your skills.
